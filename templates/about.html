About Us


This site was produced by the Strenght After Service eam as part of the Code Institute April 2023 Hackathon

The theme of the hackathon was "Stamp Out The Stigma: Supporting Veterans Mental Health"

The team is made up of Alex, Debbie, Konstantina, Ariane, Marlon and Aiga

We have done our best in the time allowed to come up with a site that allows veterans to share their transition stories

It is hoped that these stories will be uplifting and inspirational to other veterans
