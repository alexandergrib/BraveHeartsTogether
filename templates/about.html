About Us


This site was produced by the Strenght After Service Team as part of the April 2023 Hackathon, that was provided by SODA and Code Institute.

The theme of the hackathon was "Stamp Out The Stigma: Supporting Veterans Mental Health".

The aim of this website is to create a good environment for users to add and share their own stories to the community, and maybe inspire others in some ways.

We have done our best in the time allowed to come up with a site that allows veterans to share their transition stories

It is hoped that these stories will be uplifting and inspirational to other veterans.


We hope you enjoy reading them.
